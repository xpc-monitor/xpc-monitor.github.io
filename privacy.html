XPC Monitor

Privacy Policy

Effective date:

XPC Monitor is designed with privacy by default. The application observes XPC connection metadata locally to help users understand inter‑process communication on their own devices.

Key Principles

  • No data leaves your device. The app does not send, upload, or share information with any server.
  • No user tracking or analytics. We do not collect identifiers, behavioral data, or build profiles.
  • Local processing only. All event processing and filtering happen on‑device.
  • Minimal permissions. The app requests only the capabilities required to observe XPC connections.
  • User control. You can pause monitoring at any time and clear observed events from the UI.

Scope Limitation

Full Disk Access is used solely to subscribe to XPC connection notifications. The app does not interfere with the system, block any events, or access any files on disk.

Third‑party Services

We do not integrate third‑party analytics, advertising, or telemetry services.
