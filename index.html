Purpose

XPC Monitor enables privacy‑respecting visibility into macOS XPC connections.

  • Map dependencies between your app and XPC services
  • Optimize performance by removing unnecessary connections
  • Analyze connection frequency to uncover bottlenecks
  • Validate that apps connect only to expected services
  • Spot unusual patterns that may indicate suspicious activity

Features

Familiar experience

Designed to feel familiar to Console.app users. Provides powerful filtering and search tokens.

Anomaly detection

Identifies unusual XPC connection patterns that may indicate suspicious activity.

Privacy and safety

XPC Monitor runs locally, does not transmit data off‑device, and requests only the permissions required to observe XPC connections. Read our Privacy Policy.
